Features

Everything you need to
model any loan

Built for lenders, brokers, and finance teams who need accuracy, flexibility, and audit-ready output.

Amortisation Schedules

Daily accrual with monthly, financial-year, and custom period views. Supports P&I, interest-only, and payable-at-end structures with balloon residuals.

Rate Modelling

Fixed, variable, or base+margin with scheduled changes and live RBA cash rate integration.

Transactions

Drawdowns, repayments, and movements with auto-recalculation on every change.

Payment Holidays & Default Interest

Full or principal-only holidays, non-accrual periods, and default interest — with configurable capitalisation and PIK rates for complex facility structures.

Term Sheets

Print-ready term sheets generated instantly from your parameters.

Excel Export

Styled .xlsx with full schedule detail, ready for downstream analysis.

Cloud Sync

Save loans to Supabase. Access from any device, any browser.
